History / Founding Story

AI Business Solutions was founded in 2022 by a team of AI researchers and full-stack developers who recognized that many small-to-midsize businesses lacked expertise to harness AI, modern web design, and SEO. In our first year, we delivered proof-of-concept AI prototypes (predictive inventory models) and launched six mobile-optimized WordPress websites. By the end of 2023, we expanded into offering end-to-end digital solutions—combining AI, custom website development, and SEO—under one roof.
